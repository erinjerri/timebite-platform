# Torus Environment

The torus environment is a future spatial expression of the Goal Loop. It should support ritual and focus without replacing the simple iOS MVP.

## Environment State

- goal_list
- daily_intent
- active_session_timer
- completion_history
- current_streak
- reflection_prompt
- monthly_completion_calendar

## Action Vocabulary

- create_goal
- set_daily_intent
- start_session
- pause_session
- complete_session
- mark_partial
- mark_skipped
- mark_rest_day
- add_reflection
